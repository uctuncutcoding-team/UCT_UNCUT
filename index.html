💜 ABOUT US ✦

WHAT IS UCT UNCUT? 🌸

A creative home for anyone passionate about art — whether you draw, write poetry, make music, design fashion or create in any form. We are a platform where your work is seen, heard and celebrated.

WHAT DO WE OFFER? ⭐

Beyond art, we keep you in the loop. From campus stories to conversations that matter — uncut, unedited, unabridged and uncensored. Come as you are. Create boldly. Speak freely.

WHAT'S THE MAG ABOUT? 💙

We're an up-and-coming UCT-based digital magazine sharing the experiences of UCT through the words and creativity of UCT students.

IS IT FASHION ONLY? 🌿

Not at all! We share photography, fashion and creativity across multiple teams. There's space for everyone here.

WHAT TEAMS EXIST? 💜

Coding, photography, writing, events & planning, and social media. No experience needed — anyone is welcome to join any team!

PHYSICAL COPY? 📖

We're digitally based — the magazine lives right here on this website, designed by our very own design and coding team (aka: this one. hiii :))

PEOPLESOFT CODE? 🗂️

We don't have one yet — we're still in the monitoring phase! Join our WhatsApp channel for all updates 🔔

HOW MUCH TO JOIN? 💸

Nothing more than your time and ideas :))
